What is GamrCard?

GamrCard is a place to keep all your gaming platform names in one place. One of the biggest problmes in the world of games is the fact there are so many accounts you have to have to play them. Then trying to add them to your friends list out of game is hardder then it needs to be. This is where GamrCard comes in to play, we give you a Wallet to keep all your platform cards in. All you have to do is fill in all the platforms you want to keep in your wallet. Then now you can send your frined a link to your wallet and they can add you on all platforms you keep in it.
